Monrovia City Chamber Of Commerce (MCCC) Site Plan

Purpose

The Monrovia city chamber of commmerce intend is to promote businesses, the city and its people, and the availability of goods and services.

Scenarios

The Monrovia city chamber of commerce or for short MCCC helps its members understand the pratical challenges faced by businesses of all sizes and speak on its behalf, with authority, to policymakers that determines decisions affecting how business operates on day-to-day basis.

Monrovia city chamber of commerce combined expertise towards one core purpose: to enable business to secure peace, prosperity and opportunity for all.
